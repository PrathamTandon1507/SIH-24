INTRODUCTION ABOUT FOLVITE TABLET

Folvite Tablet is an iron supplement that is used to manage folic acid deficiencies such as in megaloblastic anaemias (along with Vitamin B12), or deficiencies associated with reduced absorption of nutrients, and alcoholism. It contains folic acid as an active ingredient which increases the production of red blood cells.
It can also be used as adjunctive therapy in anaemias occurring during pregnancy, infancy or childhood and also in women with a history of having children with affected brain or spinal cord.
Folic acid is a complex organic acid essential for the production of red blood cells, white blood cells and platelets and for the synthesis of proteins that are linked structurally with RNA or DNA. It is also known to manage the occurrence of neural tube defects (brain or spinal cord defects) when given during pregnancy.
Take it as advised by your doctor and do not exceed the recommended daily dosage. Folvite Tablet is not recommended for use in unmanageed vitamin B12 deficiency, pernicious anaemia, or any cancerous disease. Before taking Folvite Tablet, inform your doctor if you have a folate-dependent tumour, or any disease that reduces the amount of vitamin B12 in the body. Consult your doctor if you experience any allergic reactions while taking it.

SIDE EFFECTS OF FOLVITE TABLET

Folvite Tablet may cause allergic reaction (itchy or red skin, rash), anaphylactic reaction (swollen face, lips, tongue or throat, trouble breathing or swallowing), loss of appetite, nausea, bloating, and gas in some individuals. Consult your doctor if you notice any such side effects while taking Folvite Tablet.

DOSAGE DESCRIPTION

For adults and children over 12 years: 400 mcg once daily. For pregnant women: 600 mcg once daily. Always follow the package instructions or your doctor's advice. Dosage may be adjusted based on individual health needs.
